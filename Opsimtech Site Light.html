§ 01 · Approach

Three modeling primitives. One coherent stack.

We don't pick between data and physics — we compose them. Every Opsimtech model is built from physical priors, learned components, and constraints from the governing equations of the system.

Methods

Composable building blocks across the modeling stack.

Physical AI

Models that ingest sensors, geometry, and operating conditions — and learn behavior grounded in the physics of the asset, not just correlations in the data.

Sensor-fused Edge-deployable
ML surrogates

Neural surrogates trained on high-fidelity simulation runs. They compress days of CFD or FEA into millisecond inference, enabling real-time control loops and broad design sweeps.

PINNs

Physics-informed neural networks that encode PDEs — Navier-Stokes, heat, Maxwell, electrochemistry — directly into the loss. The model can't fit data while violating physics.

§ 02 · How it works

From sensor stream & CAD to deployable model.

A single pipeline, four stages. We can plug in at any point — work with your existing data, your existing simulation tools, or greenfield from the equations up.

/ 01

Capture

Ingest sensor telemetry, CAD geometry, operating envelopes, and any existing high-fidelity simulation runs.

/ 02

Constrain

Encode the governing PDEs & conservation laws as soft constraints. Models physically cannot drift into nonsense.

/ 03

Train

Joint optimization of data fit + physics residual + boundary conditions. Iterate against your domain's accuracy targets.

/ 04

Deploy

Ship the model as a Python SDK, edge runtime, or hooked into your design tool. Versioned, observable, retrainable.
