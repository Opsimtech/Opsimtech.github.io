§ 01 · Technology

How OpSimTech Works

A three-layer architecture that fuses high-fidelity physics simulation with neural networks — and serves the result as a live digital twin.

L1

High-Fidelity Simulation

Industrial-grade CFD, SPH and DEM solvers ground the system in first-principles physics. The data layer beneath every model.

CFD SPH DEM RANS · LES · DES
L2

Physics-AI Hybrid Layer

PINNs encoded with governing equations — Navier-Stokes, energy conservation, electrochemistry. The network learns and respects physics. Multifidelity fusion blends solver runs, sensor data, and analytical priors.

PINNs Multifidelity Fusion Navier–Stokes Energy Conservation
L3

Real-Time Digital Twin

Live predictions exposed through APIs and dashboards. Designers and operators query a continuously updated model — no mesh, no solver queue, no waiting.

REST & Python SDK < 10 ms inference Sensor-fed Edge-deployable

§ 06 · Simulation Capabilities

Six composable primitives across the stack.

Use them individually for a focused project — or chained through one validated pipeline.

CFD

Computational Fluid Dynamics. RANS, LES, DES for steady & transient flows.

SPH

Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics. Free-surface, slamming, slurries, multiphase.

DEM

Discrete Element Method. Granular powders, particulate process flow, packing.

PINNs

Physics-Informed Neural Networks. PDE-residual loss embedded in the model.

Multifidelity Modeling

Blend cheap analytical models with expensive solver runs — coherently.

Data Fusion

Merge sensor streams, lab measurements, and simulation outputs into one twin.
